# Business News Aggregator

Aggregate business and tech industry news.

## Sources
- Tech blogs and news sites
- Startup funding news
- Company earnings/quarterly reports
- Industry analysis

## Usage

### Daily Brief
```
Get today's business news summary
```

### Specific Topic
```
Tech industry news this week
Startup funding news
Company earnings reports
```

### Search
```
Search for [company/industry] news
Find news about [specific topic]
```

## Output Format
- Top 5-10 important news
- Each with: title, company, key points, link
- Categorized by topic
